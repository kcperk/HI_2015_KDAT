Connecting You With Others

Start an exchange with someone - people that want to converse. Share about your interests and talk about things that matter to you.

  • Step One

    Sign up! Choose a username that would suit you. Input a password and you're good to go!

  • Step Two

    Write your first letter! Hit the compose button and you will be redirected to The Desk. You will then be asked to choose a starter question. Choose one that interests you and a person who shares the same interest will receive the letter.
